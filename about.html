Online Hackathon

About

Due to covid-19 shutting down most schools, the the Hack the Ham Hackathon was postponed until the following school year. To give those who planned on coming an alternative we offered an online hackathon where participants had a weekend to create a project that would benefit someone in quarentine.

About Us

Hack the Ham is Cheltenham High School’s (CHS) first hackathon targeted to inspiring more high school students into taking interest in computer science early on, hosted by CHS’ Computer Science Honor Society (CSHS). Our goal is to inspire, teach, and empower high school students in our community but hope to expand it to other high schools in the future. We hope our attendees walk away with an interest in comp sci and skills to help them start being active creators. We’re committed in encouraging students of all backgrounds to participate within our diverse high school.
