About Us

Hack the Ham is Cheltenham High School’s (CHS) first hackathon targeted to inspiring more high school students into taking interest in computer science early on, hosted by CHS’ Computer Science Honor Society (CSHS). Our goal is to inspire, teach, and empower high school students in our community but hope to expand it to other high schools in the future. We hope our attendees walk away with an interest in comp sci and skills to help them start being active creators. We’re committed in encouraging students of all backgrounds to participate within our diverse high school.

FAQ

How Much Does It Cost?

$0! It’s 100% free with lunch, drinks, snacks, swag, & workshops!

Who Can Participate?

Any current 9-12 graders at Cheltenham High School. Hopefully next year, we can open it to all!.

What If I Never Coded Before?

That’s no problem at all! We encourage all of CHS students with no experience at all AND those with experience to participate. We have mentors, an amazing experienced team with beginner friendly workshops!

What Should I Bring?

Student ID, laptop, charger, and positive vibes (also any extra things you need for you project)! If you only have access to a Chromebook, we have Macs available on a first come first serve basis!

Do I Need A Team?

Absolutely not! You’ll have an opportunity to meet others who share the same goal as you to make teams of up to 4- find some new pals, bring yours, or work alone.

What Can I Make?

Anything that your creative mind takes you! A website, app, game, hardware… anything! Judges will rate your projects on creativity, technical skill, polish, & utility.
