Hack The Ham

Cheltenham High Schools First Hackathon

What?

We are hosting the first Cheltenham High School’s hackathon ever: Hack the Ham. 40 attendees will collaborate and innovate to bring their ideas to reality, developing skills beyond just the field of technology. In teams of four, attendees will build an app, website, or game over 6 hours, with workshops and mentors to help along the way.

When/Where?

Cheltenham High School: 500 Rices Mill Rd, Wyncote, PA 19095
March 28, 2020 From 7 am to 3 pm

Why?

We hope our attendees walk away with an interest in computer science and skills to help them become active creators. We’re committed in encouraging students of all backgrounds to participate within our high school.

Benefits

How Hackathons Help Everyone

Connect With Youth

Hackathons are a great way for your company to interact with the local community. Many high students are often confused and scared of what the real world entails. High schools often do not provide the opportunity of exploring careers or teaching their students how to apply their knowledge beyond. For those who even the slightest interested in computer science, may be unsure of the countless occupations and applications of what they can use and do with their skills. Your company's interaction will not only spark passion in young minds, but give them guidance and set them on a path to some day work for you!
