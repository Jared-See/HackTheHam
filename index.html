What?

We are hosting the first Cheltenham High School’s hackathon ever: Hack the Ham. 40 attendees will collaborate and innovate to bring their ideas to reality, developing skills beyond just the field of technology. In teams of four, attendees will build an app, website, or game over 6 hours, with workshops and mentors to help along the way.

When/Where?

Cheltenham High School: 500 Rices Mill Rd, Wyncote, PA 19095
March 28, 2020 From 8 am to 4 pm

Why?

We hope our attendees walk away with an interest in computer science and skills to help them become active creators. We’re committed in encouraging students of all backgrounds to participate within our high school.

Highlights

Beginner Friendly

No experience required to attend Hack the Ham. We host three beginning workshops to introduce you to web development, Python, and Java. Plus, there will be college mentors from local universities to teach you!

Fun, Fun, and More Fun!

A hackathon doesn’t entail just coding, coding, and coding! Meet new people from school who share the same interest as you and didn’t even know! They’ll be creative games for breaks and Kahoots to win prizes..

It’s free!

Show up on a Saturday with your friends for an amazing time awaiting you with food and some SWAG :)

FAQ

How Much Does It Cost?

$0! It’s 100% free with lunch, drinks, snacks, swag, & workshops!

Who can participate?

Any current 9-12 graders at Cheltenham High School. Hopefully next year, we can open it to all!

What if I never coded before?

That’s no problem at all! We encourage all of CHS students with no experience at all AND those with experience to participate. We have mentors, an amazing experienced team with beginner friendly workshops!

What should I bring?

Student ID, laptop, charger, and positive vibes (also any extra things you need for you project)! If you only have access to a Chromebook, we have Macs available on a first come first serve basis!

Do I need a team?

Absolutely not! You’ll have an opportunity to meet others who share the same goal as you to make teams of up to 4- find some new pals, bring yours, or work alone.

What can I make?

Anything that your creative mind takes you! A website, app, game, hardware… anything! Judges will rate your projects on creativity, technical skill, polish, & utility.

Who runs this?

Your fellow high school officers in Computer Science Honor Society along with the help of our awesome teacher Mrs. Putterman! The event will be supervised by her and other adults (with background checks).

Who Can I Talk To?

Any questions or concerns? Please feel free to email hacktheham@gmail.com with your personal email or sputterman@cheltenham.org
